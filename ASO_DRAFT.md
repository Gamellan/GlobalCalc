## Short description (Play Store)
All-in-one calculator for tips, currency conversion, discounts, and percentages.

## Long description (Play Store)
GlobalCalc is a fast and practical utility app designed for daily math in real life.

Use it for:
- Tip calculations for restaurants and travel
- Currency conversion with live rates and offline cache fallback
- Discount calculations while shopping
- Percentage calculations for school, work, and finance

Why users keep it installed:
- No account required
- No custom backend dependency
- Lightweight and quick interface
- Useful every day in any country

Monetization:
- Non-intrusive banner ads
- Interstitial ads during repeated conversion actions
